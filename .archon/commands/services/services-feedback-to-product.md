# Services Feedback To Product

**Input**: $ARGUMENTS

---

## Mission

Convert customer feedback into product-ready intake without losing customer context.

## Process

1. Extract customer context, problem, workaround, urgency, and business impact.
2. Identify whether the feedback is a bug, feature, enablement gap, or service request.
3. Write `$ARTIFACTS_DIR/services/feedback-to-product.md`.

## Artifact Format

```markdown
# Services Feedback To Product

## Customer Context

## Problem

## Evidence

## Workaround

## Impact

## Recommended Product Intake
```

## Output

Return the artifact path and recommendation.
